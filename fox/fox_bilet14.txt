* BILETUL 14
* Baza de date: MAGAZIN_INSTRUMENTE
* Câmpuri: Instrument (C), Client (C), Pret (N), Data (D)

USE MAGAZIN_INSTRUMENTE

* a) Instrumentele care contin caracterul 'n'
LIST FOR "n" $ LOWER(Instrument)

* b) Clientii care au achizitionat o vioara
CLOSE DATABASES
USE MAGAZIN_INSTRUMENTE
LIST Client, Instrument, Pret, Data FOR UPPER(TRIM(Instrument)) = "VIOARA"

* c) Preturile cuprinse intre 1000 si 1500 lei
CLOSE DATABASES
USE MAGAZIN_INSTRUMENTE
LIST Instrument, Client, Pret, Data FOR Pret >= 1000 .AND. Pret <= 1500

CLOSE DATABASES
